Peers Club

Professional Networking & Matching Platform

Timeline Mar 2024 — Dec 2024
Role Founder & Freelancer
Team Solo Founder

Overview

As Founder of Peersclub, I built a professional networking platform from the ground up, focusing on solving real user problems through research-driven product development. The platform connects professionals through intelligent matching algorithms and streamlined meeting coordination, while I also freelanced with startups on product management and UI/UX design.

The Challenge

Professionals seeking meaningful peer connections often struggle with finding the right matches based on availability, interests, and requirements. The challenge was to create a platform that not only matched users intelligently but also facilitated seamless introductions, scheduling, and calendar integration to convert matches into actual meetings.

My Approach

User Research & Competitive Analysis

Conducted comprehensive user research and competitive analysis to understand the core customer problems in professional networking. This research formed the foundation for feature prioritization and product strategy.

Matching Algorithm Development

Developed an intelligent matching algorithm that considers user availability, professional interests, and specific requirements to connect the right peers. The algorithm optimizes for compatibility and mutual benefit.

Invitation & Scheduling System

Implemented a comprehensive invitation system featuring user introductions, meeting scheduling, and calendar integration to reduce friction and increase the conversion rate from match to meeting.

Product Management & UI/UX Consulting

Freelanced with multiple startups, providing product management expertise and UI/UX design services. This work included defining product strategies, creating user flows, and designing intuitive interfaces that drive user engagement.

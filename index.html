About me

I majored in Engineering at MVJCE, Bangalore. During my time there, I discovered my passion for building products that solve real problems and create meaningful impact for users.

After college, I became a Product Manager passionate about user experience, design, analytics, and growth. I led the native iOS and Android teams at Meesho, where I worked on improving app engagement and conversion rates through data-driven experimentation. I then moved to MFine & Lifecell, where I led the merger transition and built B2B2C digital solutions. Most recently, I was at Peers Club as Founder, developing matching algorithms and networking platforms.

I'm currently a Product Manager at Assetworks AI, a fintech startup focused on delivering AI-powered financial insights for investors and analysts across multiple asset classes.

My goal as a Product Manager is to always work on interesting challenges that, if solved, can have a huge impact on the world. I'm particularly passionate about leveraging AI-driven technologies, data analysis, and user research to build products that drive measurable growth.

In 2024, I started coaching aspiring PMs about product management and the tech industry. I aim to help people transition into product management roles by sharing what I've learned over the years. Expect to find guidance about the product management role, industry trends, and career advice.

Outside of Product Management and Tech, I'm passionate about exploring generative AI tools like Claude Code, Cursor, and automation platforms. I enjoy coding with AI assistance, building side projects, and staying at the forefront of emerging technologies. I also love experimenting with data analytics using tools like MixPanel, Clevertap, and Power BI.
